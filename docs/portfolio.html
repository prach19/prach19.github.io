Dine Line

Dine Line is a solution to the common university problem: not having enough time to eat. Through careful research, we found that most university students skip meals when they are on campus becuase the lines are simply too long. Dine Line is an app that displays the estimated time one would spend in the line for a restaurant to help students find the right place to eat.
